PRUDENCE   الحزم

الحزم

Prudence

1ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الحزم كياسة.

1- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘Prudence is sagacity.’[Bihar al-Anwar, v. 71, p. 339, no. 8]

2ـ الإمام علي (ع) : من أخذ بالحزم استظهر ، من أضاع الحزم تهور.

2- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘He who adopts prudence is cautious, and he who lacks prudence is reckless.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, nos. 7913, 7914]

3ـ الإمام الصادق (ع) : الحزم مشكاة الظن .

3- Imam al-Sadiq (AS) said, ‘Prudence is the lamp-niche of suspicion.’[Tuhaf al-`Uqoul, no. 356]

النظر في عواقب الامور

Looking into the Consequences of Matters

4ـ الإمام علي (ع) : من تورط في الامور بغير نظر في العواقب فقد تعرض للنوائب .

4- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘He who involves himself in matters without considering the consequences exposes himself to troubles.’[Tuhaf al-`Uqoul, no. 90]

5ـ الإمام علي (ع) : التدبير قبل العمل يؤمنك من الندم.

5- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘Planning before action will preserve you from regret.’[`Uyoun Akhbar al-Ridha (AS), v. 2, p. 54, no. 204]

6ـ الإمام علي (ع) : أعقل الناس أنظرهم في العواقب.

6- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The most intelligent of people is he who looks further into the consequences.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 3367]

الحزم والعزم

Firm Resolution and Determination

7ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الظفر بالحزم والجزم .

7- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘Victory comes through firm resolution and determination.’[`Awali al-La’ali, v. 1, p. 292, no. 164]

8ـ الإمام علي (ع) : لا خير في عزم بلا حزم.

8- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘There is no good in determination without a firm resolution.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 10682]

تفسير الحزم

The Interpretation of Prudence

9ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الحزم النظر في العواقب ، ومشاورة ذوي العقول.

9- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘Prudence is careful consideration of the consequences and consultation of people of reason.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 1915]

10ـ الإمام علي (ع) : أصل الحزم الوقوف عند الشبهة.

10- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The essence of prudence is stopping in the face of the dubious.’[Tuhaf al-`Uqoul, no. 214]

11ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الطمأنينة قبل الخبرة خلاف الحزم.

11- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘Feeling certainty and quietude before experience is against prudence.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 1514]

الحازم

The Prudent

12ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الحازم من لم يشغله غرور دنياه عن العمل لاخراه .

12- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The prudent one is he who is not preoccupied by the delusion of this world from working for his Hereafter.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 1984]

13ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الحازم من تخير لخلته ؛ فإن المرء يوزن بخليله.

13- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The prudent one is he who is selective in his friendships, for man is judged according to his friend.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 2026]

14ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الحازم من لا يشغله النعمة عن العمل للعاقبة .

14- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The prudent one is he whose prosperity does not stop him from working towards the end goal.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 1878]

15ـ الإمام علي (ع) : الحازم من يؤخر العقوبة في سلطان الغضب، ويعجل مكافأة الإحسان اغتناما لفرصة الإمكان .

15- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The prudent one is he who puts off punishment when anger prevails, and hastens to reward the good, taking the first possible opportunity to do so.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 2179]

أحزم الناس

The Most Prudent of People

16ـ رسول الله‏ (ص) : أحزم الناس أكظمهم للغيظ.

16- The Prophet (SAWA) said, ‘The most prudent of people is he who is best at controlling his rage.’[Amali al-Saduq, p. 28, no. 4]

17ـ رسول الله (ص) : إن أكيسكم أكثركم ذكرا للموت ، وإن أحزمكم أحسنكم استعدادا له.

17- The Prophet (SAWA) said, ‘The most sagacious one from among you is he who remembers death the most, and the most prudent one from among you is he who is the most prepared for it.’[`alam al-Din, no. 333]

18ـ الإمام علي (ع) : أحزمكم أزهدكم.

18- Imam Ali (AS) said, ‘The most prudent one from among you is the one who is most abstemious.’[Ghurar al-Hikam, no. 2832]
